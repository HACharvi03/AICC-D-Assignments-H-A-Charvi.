1 Google Search:

Uses ranking algorithms trained on billions of searches to predict which pages are most relevant based on keywords, user behavior, and context.

2 YouTube Recommendations:

Uses collaborative filtering and deep learning to suggest videos based on your watch history and similar users’ behavior.

3 Instagram Feed & Reels:

ML models rank posts by predicting what you’re most likely to engage with using past likes, shares, and watch time.

4 Amazon Product Suggestions:

Recommendation systems analyze browsing history, purchases, and similar customers to predict what you might buy next.

5 Google Maps Traffic Prediction:

Uses historical traffic data + real-time GPS data to predict congestion and estimate arrival times using regression models.

6 Netflix Movie Recommendations:

Uses matrix factorization and deep learning to match user preferences with content features.

7 Gmail Spam Detection:

Uses classification algorithms trained on millions of emails to predict whether a message is spam or legitimate.

8 Spotify Music Recommendations:

Uses audio feature analysis + collaborative filtering to predict songs you will like.

9 Google Assistant / Amazon Alexa:

Uses speech recognition (deep neural networks) to convert voice to text and NLP models to understand intent.

10 PhonePe / Google Pay Fraud Detection:

Uses anomaly detection models to identify unusual transaction patterns and prevent fraud in real time.
